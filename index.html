Welcome to The Mammalion

The Mammalion makes the best dishes in the Buckhead community of Atlanta, Georgia. Our Atlanta inspired restaurant is located on Peachtree Street and it is a favorite for the locals in Buckhead, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, and the surrounding areas. Craving chicken? Maybe it’s time to try something a little more unique. We’re known for our homemade grilled and spiced with bold combinations. And if we can make chicken special, just imagine what we can do with a munchie, salad, calzone, or even a burger. Are you hungry yet? Definitely. Take your pick from our selection of 37 seasonal craft beers and our tasty, handcrafted cocktails. And, many of our dishes can be made gluten-free or vegan
